Welcome to COMP 3104, the comprehensive DevOps course designed to equip you with the 
skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the world of software development and IT operations. 
In this course, you'll delve deep into the principles and practices of DevOps, a set of practices that emphasizes collaboration, communication, automation, and integration between software developers and IT professionals. 
DevOps aims to shorten the software development lifecycle and deliver high-quality software continuously.


Introduction to DevOps:

Understand the evolution of DevOps and its significance in modern software development.
Explore the core principles of collaboration and communication between development and operations teams.
