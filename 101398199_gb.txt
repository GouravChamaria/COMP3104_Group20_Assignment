Location: Downtown Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
History: Founded in 1967, named after George Brown, a Father of Confederation.
Programs: Offers a wide range of diploma, degree, and certificate programs
Three Campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront.
Reputation: Known for its strong focus on practical, hands-on education.
Industry Connections: Collaborates with local businesses and industries.
Diversity: Attracts students from around the world, promoting cultural diversity.
